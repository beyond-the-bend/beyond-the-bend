## **Choosing How You Want to Move Through Winter**

As our Winter Session begins this week, I want to speak plainly about what this moment really is and what it is not.

There is no rush to be here.  
You are always welcome to join a class.  
You can step into this space at any point during the season.

What *is* closing now is the opportunity to commit to the full Winter Session as a container.

The twelve week session was created for those who want rhythm, continuity, and support through the long arc of winter and into early spring. It is for those who know that what actually helps is not an occasional class, but a steady practice they can return to week after week.

This is not about urgency.  
It is about intention.

**What the Winter Session Offers**

Twelve weeks of consistent movement and rest  
A steady nervous system rhythm rather than scattered effort  
A way to be held through winter instead of pushing yourself through it

You are not signing up to improve yourself.  
You are choosing self preservation.

**A Gentle Truth**

You do not need to decide everything today.  
You do not need to commit forever.

But if you know you want the support of the full winter arc rather than dropping in when you are already depleted, this is the moment to choose that structure.

You can explore the session options and join in a way that feels honest and sustainable for you.

If your body has been asking for something steadier this winter, this is your invitation to listen.
