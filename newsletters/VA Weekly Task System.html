Current Sprint: Video Processing (Weeks 1-2)

Until all 12 class recordings are polished and uploaded to GHL, video processing is Dianne's primary focus. Our goal is to clear this backlog and build a solid rhythm in our working relationship.

Phase 1: Setup & Housekeeping (Days 1-2)

  • Shared Drive Access: Confirm access to Google Drive and GoHighLevel (GHL).
  • Tool Setup: Download CapCut Desktop.
  • Design: Create Intro/Outro templates in Canva and **select/save royalty-free music** for the intro.
  • The Tracker: Set up the shared Google Sheet for Video Tracking as outlined in the SOP.

Expected Output

  • ~2 finished videos per day
  • ~8 to 10 videos per week
  • All 12 videos done in approximately 6 to 8 working days

Dianne's Daily Sprint Routine

  1. Download the next video from the shared Google Drive folder
  2. Open it in CapCut Desktop
  3. Add branded intro (image + music)
  4. Add branded outro (image)
  5. Quality check (watch beginning and end only)
  6. Export at 1080p as MP4
  7. Name file: YYYY-MM-DD_ClassName
  8. Upload to "FOR APPROVAL" folder in Google Drive
  9. Get approval from Laura
  10. Upload to GHL membership area with title + short description
  11. Update the Video Tracker (the shared Google Sheet)
  12. Move to the next video

Reference: See the VA Video Processing SOP for full step-by-step instructions.
