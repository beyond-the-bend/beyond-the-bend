## Terminology Check
GHL uses some specific words that can be confusing:
- **Product:** This is what you are creating now. Even though it says "Course Title," think of it as a "Content Container." Your **Sanctuary** is a Product.
- **Client Portal:** This is your "Hub." All your Products (Sanctuary, Meditations, etc.) live inside this one Hub.

## 1. Create the Product
1.  Log into GoHighLevel and navigate to **Sites** > **Memberships** > **Products**.
2.  Click **+ Create Product**.
3.  Choose **"Build Your Own"**.
4.  **Course Title:** Type **The Sanctuary**.
5.  **Course Description:** Write a warm welcome (e.g., "Welcome to your digital refuge for somatic movement and grounded rest.")
6.  **Thumbnail:** Upload a calm image. This will be the "tile" people click on inside your Hub.
7.  Click **Create Product**.

## 2. Branding & Design
1.  Inside your new product, click on **Settings** in the top menu.
2.  **Product Thumbnail:** Upload a calm, brand-aligned image (Dianne can help with this later if needed).
3.  **Customize:** Click the "Customize" tab.
    *   Find the **Header** section and set the height/color.
    *   Set the **Accent Color** to our Sage Green: `#A8B5A2`.
    *   Set the **Typography** to *Playfair Display* for Headings and *Lato* for Body.
4.  Click **Save**.

## 3. Create the Class Room
1.  Go back to the **Outline** tab.
2.  You will see a default Category. Rename it by clicking the edit (pencil) icon.
3.  Name it: **The Class Library** (or **Yoga Class Archive**).
4.  Below that category, click **+ Add Lesson**.
5.  Create a placeholder lesson. Title it: **"Upcoming Class: [Date]"**.
6.  You can repeat this to create 12 placeholders, or just leave one for Dianne to copy and paste.

## 4. Grant Access (The Offer)
1.  Navigate to **Memberships** > **Offers**.
2.  Click **+ Create Offer**.
3.  Title it: **The Sanctuary Access**.
4.  **Product:** Select "The Sanctuary" from the dropdown.
5.  **Price:** Set as "Free" (since it's for your existing members).
6.  Click **Create**.
