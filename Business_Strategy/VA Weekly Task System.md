### Expected Output
- ~2 finished videos per day
- ~8-10 videos per week
- **All 12 videos done in approximately 6-8 working days**

### Dianne's Daily Sprint Routine
1. Open the next project in Descript (follow the Video Processing SOP)
2. Add branded intro (image + text + music)
3. Add branded outro (image + text)
4. Run quality checklist
5. Export at 1080p as MP4
6. Name file: `YYYY-MM-DD_ClassName`
7. Upload to GHL membership area with title + short description
8. Update the Video Tracker (in the Video Processing SOP document)
9. Move to the next video

**Reference:** See `VA Video Processing SOP.md` for full step-by-step instructions.
