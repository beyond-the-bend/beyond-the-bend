# B Halfmoon Affiliate Application Draft

*Notes and drafted answers for the Shopify Collabs affiliate form.*
### Question 1: Tell us about you. What lights you up? What does your day-to-day look like?

**Drafted Response:**

I am the founder of Beyond the Bend Yoga, a practice dedicated entirely to the "unheard" woman. My work supports women in their prime who are navigating aging, transitions, burnout, and the heavy mental load of caring for everyone else. 

What lights me up is providing a deeply safe, unhurried space where my students can finally stop producing, achieving, or fixing themselves. I am passionate about nervous system safety, self-compassion, and somatic awareness over rigid fitness goals. My joy comes from watching a woman realize it is perfectly okay to just lay on her mat and breathe until her shoulders finally drop away from her ears. 

My day-to-day is a blend of teaching live, intimate classes (capped at 13 students) at a local studio. I record high-quality livestream sessions for my growing digital library, and I am carefully building out an online membership designed to support women who need this work but cannot attend in person. I spend my time creating newsletters that speak truth to the exhaustion that often naturally comes at this age, developing new courses, and guiding women back to their own intuition using the very props I hope to partner with you on.
