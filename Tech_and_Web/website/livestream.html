Live Stream Yoga

For the days you need to pause, but can't get away.

Practicing from home removes the pressure of getting somewhere on time while still offering the support of a live class. Livestream yoga allows you to move, breathe, and settle into your body in a way that fits your real life, exactly as it is.

You'll be guided in real time, with space to show up comfortably and without expectations.

Weekly Rhythm

The Livestream Sanctuary

Somatic Vinyasa

Mondays  ·  6:00 to 7:15 PM

A mindful flow where breath and movement connect. Instead of striving for shapes, you'll explore how your body feels from the inside out, releasing tension and moving naturally into stillness.

Rise & Restore

Tuesdays  ·  10:00 to 11:15 AM

A gentle morning practice to wake up your body with care. Supported postures and soft movement invite deep rest and renewal, soothing the nervous system so you can start your day grounded.

Restore & Reclaim

Tuesdays  ·  6:00 to 7:15 PM

An evening wind down designed to help you release the day. Through restorative poses, breathwork, and intentional rest, you'll reclaim a sense of calm and come home to yourself.

Somatic Hatha

Wednesdays  ·  10:00 to 11:15 AM

Slow, steady movement through foundational poses. Somatic Hatha builds balance, strength, and ease while leaving you grounded and refreshed.

Real Connection

The energy of a live class. The comfort of your own space.

This isn't a pre-recorded video. When you log in, you are joining a real circle of women gathering at Rising Moon Studio in Sherwood Park, AB. You get to borrow the energy of the room without leaving your home.

If you can click a link, you can do this.

You don't need fancy equipment or a special microphone. Just you and your mat.

1

Reserve your spot

Choose a class pass above and select the class that works for you.

2

Check your email

A Zoom link will arrive in your inbox before class. Simple as that.

3

Click join

From your phone, tablet, or computer. Roll out your mat and we'll be there with you.

You don't need any special setup or technical experience to join. If something unexpected comes up, you're always welcome to join another livestream class that week instead.
