The Rising Moon Sessions

Canva Design Checklist

I. Designing the 4 Intros

You will create one Canva project and duplicate it for each of these 4 class names:

  • Rising Moon | The Sessions: Restorative Yoga
  • Rising Moon | The Sessions: Somatic Hatha
  • Rising Moon | The Sessions: Somatic Vinyasa
  • Rising Moon | The Sessions: Gentle Hatha

II. The Intro Structure (3 Scenes)

Scene 1 (6 Seconds) The Welcome & Framing

Main Title: The Rising Moon Sessions

Subtitle: [Class Style Name]

"An authentic, unedited recording from our time together at Rising Moon Studio. Permission to arrive exactly as you are."

Audio: Soft music, **Fade In** over 3s.

Scene 2 (7 Seconds) Gathering Props

Text: "Take a quiet moment to gather what you need: a blanket, a pillow, or blocks. Choose anything that helps you feel supported."

Scene 3 (6 Seconds) Settling In

Text: "To begin, find a lying down position. There is nothing to fix, nothing to rush. We will begin in a moment."

Audio: Music **Fade Out** over last 3s.

III. The Outro (1 Scene)

The Parting Wish

Text: "Rest well. Your presence is a gift."

Closing: "With warmth and gratitude, Laura ❤️"

Website: beyondthebendyoga.ca

Audio: Music **Fade In** at start, **Fade Out** at last second.

IV. Canva Tech Settings

  • Size: 1920 x 1080 px (YouTube Template).
  • Fonts: Playfair Display & Lato.
  • Background: Sage Green (#A8B5A2) or Warm Sand (#E6D5C7).
  • Export: MP4 Video, Name them: Restorative_Intro.mp4, Gentle_Hatha_Intro.mp4, etc.
