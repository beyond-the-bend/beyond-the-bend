# 🌿 THE SUSTAINABLE SANCTUARY ROADMAP
**Building your Digital House, One Stone at a Time.**

Laura, this roadmap is designed to honor your nervous system. We are not "launching a funnel"—we are "tending a garden." There is no rush, only steady, unhurried progress.

---

## 🧭 PHASE 1: The Foundation (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** Organize what you already have. No "new" creation yet.
*   **Action:** Take those recordings from the last few months and drop them into the `01_Video_Library` folders I created for you.
*   **Action:** Don't worry about titles or descriptions yet. Just get the files into their "rooms."
*   **The "Anti-Burnout" Rule:** Only move 2 videos a day. Spend 15 minutes max.

## 🎨 PHASE 2: The Creative Spark (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** Record the "Shorts" and "Audios" that excite you.
*   **Action:** When you have a burst of energy, record one 5-minute meditation or a somatic short.
*   **Action:** Use our **Video Processor** to brand it in 10 seconds.
*   **The "Anti-Burnout" Rule:** If you don't feel "Wise or Wild" today, don't record. The library can wait for your best energy.

## 🛠️ PHASE 3: The Walls & Roof (Week 5-6)
**Goal:** I (the AI) and potentially your son build the actual portal.
*   **Action:** I will take the mockup you liked and turn it into a real, private website.
*   **Action:** We will "link" your folders to the website so your videos appear automatically.
*   **The "Anti-Burnout" Rule:** You do 0% of the technical work. Your job is just to look at it and say "I love this" or "Make it softer."

## 🕊️ PHASE 4: Opening the Gates (Week 7+)
**Goal:** Invite your first few steady students.
*   **Action:** Send a simple email to your inner circle inviting them to the "Pre-Launch Sanctuary" at a special rate.
*   **Action:** Let them find the bugs with us. It doesn't have to be perfect on Day 1.
*   **The "Anti-Burnout" Rule:** We build in "Margins." If life gets busy, we push the dates. The river doesn't care about a deadline.
