Time for You

Welcome, friend. If you’ve been running on fumes all week, this is your permission slip to stop. I’ve personally selected these items because they support a gentle, safe, and real practice—especially as our bodies begin to feel a bit more creaky or tired.

These aren't just props; they are tools to help you come home to yourself. I hope they bring as much grounding and peace to your mat as they have to mine.
