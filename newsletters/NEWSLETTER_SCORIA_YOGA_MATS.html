Practice of the Week: Grounding Through the Hands

This week, as we think about our connection to the earth (and those lovely cork mats!), try this simple grounding practice:

Wherever you are, place your hands flat on a surface—your mat, a table, or even your lap. Notice the texture. Is it smooth? Warm? Cool?

As you breathe in, imagine you are drawing stability up through your palms. As you breathe out, let your shoulders drop away from your ears.

Simply being aware of the physical ground beneath you can be a powerful way to quiet a busy mind.
