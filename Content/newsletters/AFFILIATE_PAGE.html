Scoria World

Sustainable cork yoga mats and meditation products
Woman Owned Sustainable Small Business Cork & Natural Materials

I have to tell you about Scoria because of something that happens in my studio almost every week. One of my students brings in her Scoria cork mat, and without fail, someone walks over, touches it, and says "where did you get this?" It is genuinely beautiful. The texture, the colour, the way it looks and feels. She gets a kick out of it every time, and honestly so do I.

I have not personally used their products yet (full transparency), but I have done my research and I love what this company stands for. Scoria is a woman owned small business that creates sustainably harvested cork yoga mats, bolsters, and meditation products. Cork provides natural grip that actually improves when your hands get warm, it is antimicrobial, and the trees are not cut down during harvesting. They regenerate. A portion of Scoria's proceeds also supports Indigenous youth through play based education, and all their deliveries are carbon neutral.

Many of you have been asking about bolsters for your home practice, and Scoria makes beautiful natural and organic yoga bolsters filled with buckwheat hulls. They come in several colours, the firmness is adjustable (you can add or remove the fill), and the covers are removable for washing. They ship free across Canada.

Why I Recommend Scoria

  • I have seen their cork mat up close every week in my studio. The quality speaks for itself
  • Woman owned, small batch, made with real intention
  • Sustainably harvested cork. No trees are harmed in the process
  • Their bolsters are organic, adjustable, and beautifully made
  • A portion of proceeds supports Indigenous youth
  • Carbon neutral shipping, free across Canada
  • GOTS certified (fair trade and organic) fabrics and non toxic dyes
