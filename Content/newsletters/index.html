The Story

A mat next to yours.

I'm not here to preach from a mountaintop. I'm 55, I'm tired of the hustle, and my body feels different than it used to. I started Beyond the Bend because I needed a place where I didn't have to "perform" wellness.

In our Sherwood Park studio, we tell the truth about how we're doing. And then we move, we breathe, and we find a way back to ourselves.
